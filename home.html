Welcome to An Cua

"At An Cua, we not only bring the distinctive flavors of crab and shrimp but also create a unique culinary experience through the perfect combination with exquisite sauces. With respect for nature and the richness of Vietnam's seas, we hope that each dish here will take diners on a surprising and delightful journey of taste exploration."

Cozy Atmosphere

With a capacity of 20-30 guests, the first floor of An Cua is ready to welcome those who wish to book events with us. We cater to parties for companies, office groups, meetings, family gatherings, birthdays, and more.

A cozy space with pleasant, bright tones, alongside a wooden window frame overflowing with flowers and greenery outside the balcony. It promises to become an ideal, warm gathering place.

An Cua cuisine

At An Cua, we take pride in offering you fresh seafood delicately prepared with our special sauces. Each sauce is meticulously crafted, from spicy to sweet, to enhance the natural flavors of the fresh seafood. We are committed to providing diners with a complete culinary experience where every bite is rich and full of emotion.
